Near range

WiFi or Bluetooth Low Energy allow your hardware to easily connect to your local network or talk directly to your computers, tablets or smartphones.

Long range

With new cellular technologies like Narrowband IoT (NB-IoT), LTE-M or 5G your hardware can send data for a long time without the need for an infrastructure.

Low power

Components consuming low power combined with energy harvesting techniques enable your hardware to last longer than just a few weeks (or a few years).
